Description:
Demo videos of the system. Shown is the pose estimation accuracy, the novice following AR demonstrations, and the AR overlay during camera motion. The calibration procedure is also shown. Below is a detailed list of the supplementary videos:
Supplementary Movie S1: Real-time AR overlay demonstrating pose estimation accuracy of articulated instruments.
Supplementary Movie S2: Novice shadowing the AR expert demonstrations on the pick-and-place task.
Supplementary Movie S3: Novice shadowing the AR expert demonstrations on the single-handed wire-chaser task.
Supplementary Movie S4: Playback of expert demonstration on pick-and-place task with moving ECM.
Supplementary Movie S5: Playback of expert demonstration on single-handed wire-chaser task with moving ECM.
Supplementary Movie S6: Calibration procedure.
Supplementary Movie S7: Pose estimation (last joint PSM1) demo.
