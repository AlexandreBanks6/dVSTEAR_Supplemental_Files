Description:
Appendix for dV-STEAR and demo videos of the system. Shown is the pose estimation accuracy, the novice following AR demonstrations, and the AR overlay during camera motion. The calibration procedure is also shown. Below is a detailed list of the supplementary materials:
Supplementary Appendix S1: Gives details on the back-propagation tool rendering and the ECM motion tracking methods as well as results from the User Engagement Scale short form survey.
Supplementary Movie S2: Real-time AR overlay demonstrating pose estimation accuracy of articulated instruments.
Supplementary Movie S3: Novice shadowing the AR expert demonstrations on the pick-and-place task.
Supplementary Movie S4: Novice shadowing the AR expert demonstrations on the single-handed wire-chaser task.
Supplementary Movie S5: Playback of expert demonstration on pick-and-place task with moving ECM.
Supplementary Movie S6: Playback of expert demonstration on single-handed wire-chaser task with moving ECM.
Supplementary Movie S7: Calibration procedure.
Supplementary Movie S8: Pose estimation (last joint PSM1) demo.
